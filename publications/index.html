2025

  1. NAACL
    PA-RAG: RAG Alignment via Multi-Perspective Preference Optimization
    Jiayi Wu, Yukun Ren, Tianhao Luo, and 3 more authors
    In Proceedings of NAACL 2025, 2025
    Base paper for this project
    Abs Bib HTML

    Proposes multi-perspective preference alignment for RAG generators via DPO, covering informativeness, robustness, and citation quality. The direct base work for Conflict-Aware PA-RAG.

2024

  1. arXiv
    Knowledge Conflicts for LLMs: A Survey
    Rongwu Xu, Zehan Qi, Cunxiang Wang, and 3 more authors
    arXiv preprint arXiv:2403.08319, 2024
    Abs Bib HTML

    Comprehensive survey of knowledge conflict types in LLMs: context–memory, inter-context, and intra-memory conflicts. Core reference for defining the research problem scope.

  2. arXiv
    ClashEval: Quantifying the Tug-of-War Between an LLM’s Internal Prior and External Evidence
    Kevin Wu, Eric Wu, and James Zou
    arXiv preprint arXiv:2404.10198, 2024
    Abs Bib HTML

    Benchmark for measuring how LLMs balance internal knowledge vs. retrieved evidence under conflict. Used as a synthetic training data source for DPO preference pairs.

  3. arXiv
    ConflictBank: A Benchmark for Evaluating the Influence of Knowledge Conflicts in LLMs
    Zhaochen Jin, Wenlong Zhang, Shaozhi Zeng, and 3 more authors
    arXiv preprint arXiv:2408.12076, 2024
    Abs Bib HTML

    Large-scale benchmark covering context–memory and inter-context conflicts. Provides conflict pairs used for DPO training data construction in this project.

  4. arXiv
    WikiContradict: A Benchmark for Evaluating LLMs on Real-World Knowledge Conflicts from Wikipedia
    Mateusz Orlikowski, Dirk Hovy, and Barbara Plank
    arXiv preprint arXiv:2406.13805, 2024
    Abs Bib HTML

    Natural knowledge conflict benchmark from Wikipedia contradictions. Used for out-of-distribution evaluation to measure limits of conflict-aware fine-tuning.

2023

  1. NeurIPS
    Direct Preference Optimization: Your Language Model Is Secretly a Reward Model
    Rafael Rafailov, Archit Sharma, Eric Mitchell, and 3 more authors
    In Advances in Neural Information Processing Systems, 2023
    Abs Bib HTML

    Proposes DPO as a simpler and more stable alternative to RLHF for preference alignment. The core training objective used in Conflict-Aware PA-RAG.

2022

  1. ICLR
    LoRA: Low-Rank Adaptation of Large Language Models
    Edward J Hu, Yelong Shen, Phillip Wallis, and 4 more authors
    In International Conference on Learning Representations, 2022
    Abs Bib HTML

    Parameter-efficient fine-tuning via low-rank weight updates. Enables DPO training on limited academic compute by drastically reducing trainable parameters.

2020

  1. NeurIPS
    Retrieval-Augmented Generation for Knowledge-Intensive NLP Tasks
    Patrick Lewis, Ethan Perez, Aleksandra Piktus, and 4 more authors
    In Advances in Neural Information Processing Systems, 2020
    Abs Bib HTML

    Seminal RAG paper combining dense retrieval with seq2seq generation. Foundational architecture for the retrieval pipeline implemented in this project.
